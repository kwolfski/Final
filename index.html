Hey there! These dating prompts were originally conceived as a personal gift on Valentines Day 2016. After enjoying many of the dates together as a couple, we decided to share the fun! We just recently took the deck and redesigned it into its current iteration, and we're extremely happy with the end results. We can't wait to start sending these out into the world and hopefully hearing about the creative dates that ensue. Happy dating!

Coming up with a great date idea can be tough... Leave it to Date Deck to spark the flame for you! With 52 cards and 52 dates, Date Deck has a new fun prompt for every week of the year. Don't ever settle for 'Dinner & a Movie' again! Date Deck is great for all types of daters, whether its a first date or a fifth anniversary.
